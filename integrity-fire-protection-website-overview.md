### 3. Services (`/services`)

   |---|---|---|

   | **Fire Damper Testing** | Prevents fire/smoke spread through ductwork; per NFPA 80 | Regular per NFPA 80 |

3. **Inspection Frequency Reference List**
   A checklist-style section summarizing all required inspection schedules:

   - Dry systems: full trip every 3 years

   - Private fire hydrants: flow tested annually
   - Class 1 hose valves: annually
   - Class 2 hose valves: every 3 years
   - Class 3 hose valves: annually
   - Dry standpipes: hydrostatic test every 5 years
   - Backflows: annually

**Notable Strengths:**

- Clear regulatory references (NFPA 10, 13, 25, 80; CAN/ULC-536:2019; Building Code Article 6.7.16)

## Key Observations for Redesign

### What's Working
- Clear, simple navigation — easy to find what you need
- Services page is thorough and well-organized
- Regulatory/code references add authority and credibility
- YouTube video links are a nice value-add for education
- Certifications are prominently called out on the homepage

### What's Missing or Weak
- **No social proof** — no Google Reviews, testimonials, or case studies anywhere on the site
- **No team presence** — no names, photos, or bios; undermines the "family business" positioning
- **About Us is thin** — essentially repeats homepage copy; no real story
- **No after-hours or emergency contact info** — critical for a safety-related service business
- **No Google Maps embed** on Contact page
- **No email address** displayed publicly
- **Foam Systems caveat** ("techs currently getting certified") could be removed or reframed
- **Wix content duplication** artifact visible on About Us page (same text block rendered twice)
- **Mobile experience** may be inconsistent given the Wix dual-render approach observed
- **No blog or resources section** — missed opportunity for SEO and authority content (e.g., "What happens if your backflow isn't tested?")
- **Copyright footer still reads 2021** — site appears unmaintained
